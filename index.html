WCN TV
WCN-TV-Download.bat
The complete master archive — everything Wendell Charles NeSmith has released publicly, in one place: television series and long-form video essays, written works and comics, and music, podcasts, and audiobooks, spanning more than a decade. Every volume is packaged as a clean numbered zip (01-, 02-...) instead of scattered loose files. Fetches the live volume list from archive.org and lets you choose exactly what to download. Resume-safe with a live progress meter per file, and every finished download is checked against archive.org's MD5 hash — a mismatch is deleted and retried automatically. This is the recommended way to get everything, all in one organized place.
