Mirror Scripts

ATHEDEN MIRROR
Atheden-Download.bat
Mirrors the Atheden music archive — sacred songs by Ivory and Wendell. Downloads MP3, PNG, TXT, and BAT files. Lyrics (TXT) are always refreshed on every run. Album art matching an MP3 basename and spectrograms are automatically excluded. Resume-safe for audio and images.
RESUME-SAFE LYRICS ALWAYS FRESH MP3 · PNG · TXT · BAT

Mirrors the Athedia infrastructure archive — the complete technical framework, source files, and deployment configurations. Downloads ZIP and BAT files. Resume-safe, preserving all historical versions locally as natural version control.

WIKKKAN BOOKS MIRROR
Books-Download.bat
Mirrors the WIKKKAN books archive — philosophy, spirituality, psychology, politics, and personal transformation by Wendell Charles NeSmith. On run, choose your mode: wipe everything and redownload fresh (ideal when books have been updated), or resume and skip files already local.
WIPE OR RESUME YOU CHOOSE TXT · BAT

OPEN SOURCE HEART TV
OpenSourceHeartTV-Download.bat
Mirrors the complete TV, film, and course archive — 43 items spanning 2012 to present. Select any title from a chronological menu and download its MP4 files. Resume-safe with server-side size verification: partial or corrupted downloads are detected and redownloaded automatically. Archive.org derivative files are excluded.
